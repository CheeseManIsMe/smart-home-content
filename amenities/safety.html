Safety Technology

To keep your home as safe as possible from the threat of fire, it is equipped with highly advanced fire suppression systems!

Fire Safety Systems:

Fire Alarm

This highly advanced fire alarm is incredibly sensitive to even the slightest amount of smoke! This gives you an earlier warning to leave the home and call emergency resopnders.

Water Sprinklers

These water sprinklers will begin spraying water in any room that a fire alarm has gone off in. This can help slow the spread of fires or completely extinguish smaller fires!

Water Mice

These modified Sweeper Mice have had their dust collection unit replaced with a small water tank. This allows them to essentially be the very first responders in the case of a small fire in your home!

Fire Snakes

These mechanized hoses are your home's last line of defense against an out of control fire. They spray a green foam to hopefully extinguish the fire, or to at least give firefighters more time to respond!
