Safety Technology

To keep your home as safe as possible from the threat of fire, it is equipped with highly advanced fire suppression systems!

Fire Safety Systems:

Fire Alarm

These incredibly advanced fire alarms can detect even the slightest amount of smoke! This gives you an incredibly early warning to a possible home fire.

Water Sprinklers

These water sprinklers begin spraying after a fire alarm has gone off. This can help slow, or even stop, the spread of a fire!

Water Mice

These slightly modified sweeper mice have a small built in water tank that can be used to spray smaller house fires.

Foam Snakes

These mechanical hoses drop down from the attic in when a fire reaches an immense size. They use a green foam to slow down the spread of the fire to give fire fighters more time to respond.
