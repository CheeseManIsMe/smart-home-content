Home Safety Technologies

Fire Safety Systems:

To keep your home as safe as possible from the threat of fire, it is equipped with highly advanced fire suppression systems! This includes highly advanced fire fighting robots and fire alarms!

Fire Alarm

This highly advanced fire alarm is incredibly sensitive to even the slightest amount of smoke; giving you an early warning to a fire in the house!

Water Sprinklers

These water sprinklers will begin spraying water in any room that a fire alarm has gone off in. This can help slow the spread of fires or completely extinguish smaller fires!

Water Mice

These modified Sweeper Mice have had their dust collection unit replaced with a small water tank, thus allowing them to put out small fires!

Foam Snakes

These mechanized hoses are your home's last line of defense against an out of control fire by spraying a green foam to extinguish fires!
