Automatic Cleaning Services

Meet The Cleaning Team:

This home comes fully equipped with a built in squadron of cleaning robots that will keep your home sparkling clean! They can range from small sweepers; to full-blown robotic dishwashers!

Sweeper Mouse

These small robots come out at predetermined times to clean you home of any dust or dirt it may have accumulated!

Scrap Rat

These larger relatives of the sweeper mice use their powerful clamping jaw to carry larger pieces of debris to the home's incinerator!

Automatic Dish Cleaner

This robotic sink uses small grasping claw to grab and scrub your dishes while powerful water streams blast away any remaining grime or filth!
